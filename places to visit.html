My top 10 Places to visit in Lagos



Lagos is a very big city with different places to visit but as someone who has stayed in Lagos for most part of my years, I have picked my top 10 places to visit when touring Lagos.


  1. Lekki Conservation Centre
  2. Badagry Slave house
  3. La Campagne
  4. Tarkwa Bay
  5. Omu Resort
  6. Fun factory
  7. Nike Art Gallery
  8. Ikogosi Warm Spring
  9. National Theatre
  10. Freedom Park
